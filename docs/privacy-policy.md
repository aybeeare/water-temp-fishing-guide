# Privacy Policy — Water Temperature Fishing Guide

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Overview

Water Temperature Fishing Guide is an Alexa skill that provides real-time water temperature and fishing condition information for lakes, rivers, and coastal locations across North America.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect or store any personal information.**

When you use this skill, the only data processed is the location name you speak (for example, "Lake Michigan" or "Revere Beach"). This location name is used solely to retrieve publicly available water temperature data from third-party sources (USGS, NOAA, and seatemperature.info). It is not stored, logged, or associated with your identity.

## Amazon Shopping Cart

If you choose to add a fishing gear recommendation to your Amazon cart, this action is handled entirely by Amazon's in-skill purchasing system. We do not have access to your Amazon account, order history, or payment information. Gear recommendations may generate a small commission through the Amazon Associates program at no additional cost to you.

## Third-Party Data Sources

Water temperature data is retrieved in real time from:

- **USGS National Water Information System** — usgs.gov
- **NOAA Tides and Currents** — tidesandcurrents.noaa.gov
- **Sea Temperature Info** — seatemperature.info

We do not control these services and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

## Nearby Bait Shop

If you request a nearby bait shop, your approximate location (based on the water body you asked about) may be used to query the Google Places API. No precise device location is accessed. Google's privacy policy applies to this lookup: policies.google.com/privacy

## Children's Privacy

This skill is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Any updates to this policy will be reflected on this page with a revised date.
